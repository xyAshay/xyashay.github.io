Hello World or 1.e4?
Jan 7, 2025
General
Chess
Random thoughts, tech tinkering, chess stuff and who knows what else.🌍💻

Hey there! Welcome to this shiny new project. Honestly, I never really planned to start writing — I just had some downtime at work, got a little bored, and thought, “Why not build a blog?” So, here we are, with my very post.

Why Blog?

“Why would a dev, who can probably spend hours tinkering with code, decide to start blogging? Was this just a whimsical project born out of sheer boredom?” -Absolutely.

“Will I keep this up?” -Who knows.

Let’s face it— half the time, we’re neck-deep in code or debugging things that should’ve worked hours ago. But the other half of the time? There’s that creative itch. Maybe I’ll write about tech. Maybe I’ll throw in a random post about the best coffee for marathon coding sessions. Or, I could just forget about this blog completely after today. The possibilities are endless, but also very unclear.

Where to Next?

“So what’s the plan?” -No idea.

“Will I post again?” -Maybe.

This could become a dev diary, a series of rants about obscure bugs, or a graveyard for unfinished ideas. But for now, I’m just here, saying, “Hello, world,” and seeing where this experiment takes me.

Catch you later… or not? Let’s find out.
